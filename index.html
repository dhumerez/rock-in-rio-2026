🚌 Getting to Cidade do Rock

Cidade do Rock is in Barra da Tijuca (Av. Salvador Allende, 6555). 📍 View on Google Maps →

🚌 BRT TransOeste

Dedicated bus rapid transit corridor. Best public transport option.

  • Nearest station: Riocentro or Terminal Jardim Oceânico
  • Fare: ~R$4.30 per trip
  • From Barra: 15–25 min
  • From Zona Sul: Metro → Jardim Oceânico + BRT (50–70 min)
  • On concert days: extended hours, extra buses

💡 Essential Tips & Resources

🛂 Documents Bolivians need a valid passport to enter Brazil. No visa required for tourism (up to 90 days). Check passport expiry — must be valid 6+ months from entry.
Brazilian Embassy in La Paz →
💳 Money Brazilian Real (R$). Cards widely accepted. Carry some cash for street vendors. Exchange rate ~R$5.83/USD.
Wise (best FX rates) →  |  Live USD→BRL rate →
📱 Connectivity Buy prepaid SIM at airport or Claro/TIM/Vivo store (~R$30 for 10GB). eSIM: Airalo or Holafly ($15–25 for 10 days).
Airalo Brasil eSIM →  |  Holafly Brasil →
🔒 Safety Don't flash valuables. Use Uber (not street taxis). Avoid empty streets at night. Keep phone in front pocket. Barra is very safe; Zona Sul requires more awareness.
Uber Rio →
☀️ Weather September = late winter / early spring. 20–28°C (68–82°F). Light jacket for evenings. Some rain possible.
Rio September weather →
🎒 Concert Day Survival Comfortable sneakers. Power bank. Cidade do Rock is HUGE (15+ min walks between stages). Water refill stations available. No outside food/drinks allowed. Lockers available for rent.
🗣️ Language Portuguese, not Spanish! Basics: "Obrigado" = thanks, "Por favor" = please, "Quanto custa?" = how much? Many Cariocas understand some Spanish.
